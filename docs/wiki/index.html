What is locationjoystick?

locationjoystick lets you spoof your GPS location on Android — no root required. Use a floating joystick, saved routes, or automatic roaming to move your location anywhere while your other apps keep running normally.

It uses Android's official mock location API. Enable Developer Options, select locationjoystick as your mock location provider, and every app on the device sees the location you choose.

Screens

Home

Main hub. Start, stop, and navigate to all features.

Map

OpenStreetMap view. Tap to walk or teleport. Launch routes, favorites, and roaming from here.

Routes

Create, edit, and replay saved waypoint routes. Straight-line or road-following.

Favorites

Save named locations. Teleport or walk there instantly.

Settings

Speed profiles, GPS realism toggles, widget config, export/import, and QR transfer.

Overlays

Floating joystick and widget that stay on top of any app.

Onboarding

First-run setup: permissions, mock location provider selection.

Quick start

  1. Install the app and open it.
  2. Follow the onboarding flow: grant location permission, overlay permission, and set locationjoystick as your mock location app in Developer Options.
  3. Tap the Map card, tap the start button, then long-press the map to walk or teleport anywhere.
  4. Use the floating widget or joystick overlay to control your location while other apps are in the foreground.
